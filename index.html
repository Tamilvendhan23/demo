Who Can Donate Blood?

Any healthy person (male and female) with good habits in the age group of 18-65 can donate blood once every 3 months regularly. During blood donation, we collect only 350/450ml from the 5 liters of blood available in the body.

Donating blood is a simple yet impactful way to contribute to society and save lives. Join us in this noble cause and make a difference today.

  • Healthy 18-65 years.
  • Minimum 50 kg.
  • No infections history.
  • No recent tattoos.
  • No major surgeries.
  • No chronic medication.
